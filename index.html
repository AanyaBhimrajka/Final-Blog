places must visit

The Eiffel Tower

In the famous known city of Paris lies the Eiffel Tower. Having visited it in 2016 I experinced the time of my life. Bustling with people and shops this monument is the greatest of all times. Taller than a skyscraper this piece of architecture is iconic! While the queues are long it is definitely worth the wait! Getting inside you see wonderous things... shops, cafes and stunning views. If I could I would visit this place again!

London

Being the capital and the largest city in England, London has the most awestrucking places to visit! The pictures of the Big Ben, the London eye, the London bridge don't lie. In fact there is even more beauty to it when you see it in person. Make sure to visit this magnificent city atleast once... when you go there don't forget to hog the shops- they have the best quality ☺

review on the movies

Harry Potter and the Half Blood Prince

If you haven't seen it you must have heard of the legendary Harry Potter series. This series is an award winning one and is one to look out for. Honestly, choosing my favorite out of these series is tremendously tough, however the 'Half Blood Prince' movie was quite appealing to me. However, you cannot see this movie unless you have read or watched the other ones. This movie is perfect for tweens and adults.

Avengers Endgame

The Avengers Endgame is a classic movie in the MCU ( Marvel cinimatic universe ). I thoroughly enjoyed watching it with my friends in the theater. A bit of violence hinders the movie for children below 8-9 years of age but makes it better for older children. I loved the storyline of the movie as it really gives you something to grasp on!
